Info

About us and the classes we offer

FlowArtes

Our aim at FlowArtes is to bring a tiny slice of Brazil to the community. To show case the different facets of Brazil as well as its culture, to help people understand and appreciate it.

Get in touch with us on Facebook or Instagram! Or feel free to drop by our place at 344C King George’s Ave (S)S208576.

We offer the following classes:

Capoeira

Capoeira is a holistic and dynamic martial art that comprises of kicks, palm strikes, elbows, takedowns and throws. It develops great stamina, strength, agility and flexibility, whilst improving one’s body and mental capabilities.

This class is a great complement to self-defence and for those doing Mixed Martial Arts.

Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu (BJJ)

Brazilian jiu-jitsu is based on the fact that an individual is able to defend themselves against a stronger or larger opponent; with the use of leverages, proper techniques, joint locks and shocks in order to control and defeat the opponent when the fight is brought to the ground.

This is a great class for those looking to build their ground game but also a perfect introduction into self-defence and the sport, as BJJ is one of the fastest growing martial arts in the world.

Movement Flow Classes

The movement class is an extension of both art forms as it helps take you through flow and mobility. Students will better understand their bodies and discover their flow in whichever martial art they are doing.

If you have no martial art background, this class is a fun way to explore the movements of your body and get more acquainted with yourself.

Conditioning Classes

This class is developed from the base of both Capoeira and BJJ. It is to help those who want to attain a better physical capacity for either of the practice or a perfect way to cross-train. If you are a practitioner of one of the martial arts and haven’t tried the other this class will help you get fitter while trying something new.

This class is also for the fitness enthusiasts who enjoy different classes and who are interested in trying a new HIIT Class that will be sure to raise your endorphin levels.

Maculelê

Maculelê is one of the traditional dances that have emerged from Brazil through Capoeira. There is a rich history to this dance and the only way to appreciate it is to be immersed in the dance and music itself.

This class brings you a mix of Afro dance movements with sticks, making it a complete workout suitable for all ages.

Music

Music plays a big part and is deep-rooted in the Brazilian culture. This class will teach you the different instruments used in Capoeira and you will learn to sing in Brazilian-Portuguese. It’s a great way to pick up a new language.
